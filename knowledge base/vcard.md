# vCard

A.K.A. _Virtual Contact File_ (VCF).<br/>
File format standard for electronic business cards.

Files can contain:

- Name.
- Address information.
- Phone numbers.
- E-mail addresses.
- URLs.
- Logos.
- Photographs.
- Audio clips.
